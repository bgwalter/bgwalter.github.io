55 Cancri A

The more massive of the two stars. 55 Cnc A is very similar to the sun although slightly smaller. Its radius is 94% that of the sun, its mass is 90-92% that of the sun, however it is only 60% as luminous. While our sun is a G2 V star, 55 Cnc A is a G8 V star. Interestingly, 55 Cnc is extremely rich in metals and is in a rare class known as “super-metal rich” stars.
