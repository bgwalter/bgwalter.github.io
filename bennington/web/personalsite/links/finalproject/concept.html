When you first arrive on the site, you will be greeted by a homepage asking you, "Where would you like to go?"

When you hover over a planet, the name of the planet will show up underneath.

It will be possible to click on the moons of each planet to get information about each of them. Every planet (and the sun) is clickable, and there will be information about dwarf planets, asteroids, and human structures.

The large question mark at the top will give general information about space travel, such as why it's important, what problems there are (distance, health, money, time), and links to where I gathered my information.
